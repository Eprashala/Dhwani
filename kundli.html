    Spashtha Lagna Kundali

    BPHS Bhava Darshan (The 12 Houses of Cosmic Karma)

    1st House (Tanu Bhava)
    The Core Self: Represents your physical body, vitality, innate nature, and general life path. It defines your ego, intellect, fame, and how the external world perceives your identity.
    Body Focus: Head, brain, and overall physical constitution.
    2nd House (Dhana Bhava)
    Resources & Values: Governs accumulated wealth, bank balance, and family lineage. It rules speech, truthfulness, early childhood nourishment, food intake, and precious metals.
    Body Focus: Face, throat, teeth, and the right eye.
    3rd House (Sahaja Bhava)
    Courage & Effort: The house of Parakrama (self-effort), courage, and physical valor. It signifies younger siblings, short journeys, communication, writing, and the use of one's hands/skills.
    Body Focus: Shoulders, arms, hands, nervous system, and the right ear.
    4th House (Matru Bhava)
    Foundation & Roots: Represents the mother, inner emotional peace, and psychological roots. It governs physical property, lands, hidden treasures, vehicles/conveyances, and primary education.
    Body Focus: Chest, heart, and lungs.
    5th House (Putra Bhava)
    Creation & Merit: Crucially holds Purva Punya (past life merit). It governs children, supreme intelligence, creativity, spiritual mantras, speculative investments, and romantic love.
    Body Focus: Stomach, upper abdomen, and liver.
    6th House (Ari Bhava)
    Obstacles & Service: The house of overcoming enemies (Shadripu), ongoing debts, and diseases. It rules daily routines, service/subordinates, pets, litigation, and physical injuries.
    Body Focus: Intestines, digestive tract, and immune system.
    7th House (Kalatra Bhava)
    Partnerships: Represents the "other," the spouse, marital harmony, and legal business partnerships. It governs public image, foreign trade, and is a key Maraka (death-inflicting) house.
    Body Focus: Lower abdomen, kidneys, and pelvic region.
    8th House (Randhra Bhava)
    Transformation & Mystery: Governs longevity, sudden events, and the mode of death. It is the realm of deep occult science, psychological rebirth, unearned wealth (inheritance/taxes), and hidden affairs.
    Body Focus: Excretory organs and reproductive system.
    9th House (Dharma Bhava)
    Wisdom & Grace: The house of righteousness, luck, and divine fortune. It signifies the father, Gurus, higher philosophical education, religion, and long spiritual pilgrimages.
    Body Focus: Thighs and hips.
    10th House (Karma Bhava)
    Action & Authority: Defines your profession, karmic duties to society, and peak career achievements. It governs fame, public status, honor, and your relationship with the government or royalty.
    Body Focus: Knees and joints.
    11th House (Labha Bhava)
    Gains & Networks: The house of massive gains, liquid income, and the ultimate fulfillment of desires. It rules elder siblings, large social networks, powerful friends, and communal organizations.
    Body Focus: Calves, ankles, and the left ear.
    12th House (Vyaya Bhava)
    Dissolution & Liberation: Governs expenses, final losses, and ultimate liberation (Moksha). It signifies foreign settlements, isolated places (ashrams/hospitals), sleep comforts, and hidden enemies.
    Body Focus: Feet and the left eye.

    🌿 Final Understanding: The Four Purusharthas (Cosmic Goals)

    🔥 1, 5, 9 → Dharma (Purpose):
    Righteousness, soul's duty, moral alignment, and spiritual growth.
    💰 2, 6, 10 → Artha (Wealth & Work):
    Material security, resource management, career, and daily survival.
    ❤️ 3, 7, 11 → Kama (Desires):
    Fulfillment of ambitions, emotional connections, partnerships, and worldly joy.
    🌌 4, 8, 12 → Moksha (Liberation):
    Inner peace, letting go, profound transformation, and ultimate freedom.
    👉 Life achieves supreme equilibrium only when all four are aligned.
